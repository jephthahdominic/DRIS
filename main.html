HISTORY

Dominican Royale was founded on the 14th of January, 2005. We started with our nursery and primary classes (nursery 1 - primary 1), we call these our pioneering classes. Its not exactly been easy but we are grateful to God for seeing us through all the way. In the year 2010, Dominican Royale introuced the secondary school classes to Her system, which has infact been really wonderful and early in 2020 the Montessori learning method was introduced. Basically the montessori learning method was adopted for the daycare and nursery classes. The montessori method of learning is a better method for teaching introduced to be able to communicate effectively with infants (children from ages 2-4 years). The school has had remarkable success and tremendous growth both academically and extra-curricular over the years.

THE SCHOOL'S MANAGEMENT

An obvious contributor to our successes so far is our up and doing Management system. Our management body consists of wonderful corporate staff with a very great zeal to work. In D.R.I.S, every student is seen as important. We have a very condusive and serene environment for learning in a very secure location.

THE SCHOOL'S LABORATORIES

We have wonderful laboratories, like our science laboratories including the Physics Laboratory, Chemistry Laboratory and the Biology Laboratory. We just introduced our montessori laboratory to improve the learning rate of all our students including the toddlers. As it is known, some people learn better by practicals which seems to be one of the most effective methods of learning if not the most effective method, some learn better by hearing which is why we have teachers who ensure they speak edibly to the hearing of their students listening. Some students also understand clearly by images. In either cases we try our best to understand the children's rate of understanding, then we try to search for images that clearly explains the topic, take them to the laboratories and even show videos concerning the topics. We believe this way the understanding rate would be faster for the children.

THE SCHOOL'S EFFECTIVE TRANSPORTATION SYSTEM

We have put a wonderful Transportation system in place to efficiently convey our students safely from home to school and vice versa. We gladly listen to the complaints of our parents/patrons on aspects we are not doing well in and by the grace of God it has helped us to improve greatly in this aspect and in the other aspects of our school.
